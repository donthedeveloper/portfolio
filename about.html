About Me

I am a freelance web developer and consultant. I'm interested in joining a team of developers - one in which I can collaborate, learn, and grow with. I've grown comfortable with the LAMP stack. Over the next few months, you will see me building more with Bootstrap, SASS, Angular, and CodeIgniter on Twitch.

Oh yeah, I live stream my development process of many projects. While I try and aim for quick, bite-sized projects, the content is driven by my viewers. I build larger-scale projects on live stream as well. I understand that there's a lot of risk in hiring new developers. Why not see what I can do for yourself?

I am self-taught. Everything I have learned comes from my love for coding and design. I started with the LAMP stack, because it was simple to learn and build basic websites. To this day, I still love it, because of how complex, robust, and powerful I have found it to be.
