About Me

I am an aspiring web developer. While I am open to projects for freelance work, I am more interested in joining a team of developers - one in which I can collaborate, learn, and grow with. By putting time and energy into creating mutually beneficial relationships, I have successfully managed teams and led departments to financial success in other fields. I would like to share that experience and apply it to a career in web development - a career in which I thought was just a dream until recently.
